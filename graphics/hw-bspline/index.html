extended bspline editor,
by melissa

new features:

  • click anywhere without a point to add a new point which will appear in green.
  • hover over any color on the left palette to preview your artwork with a different colored fill.
  • select a color to change your artwork fill color.
  • click on the upper right corner to toggle the connecting lines on and off.
  • click on the lower right corner to toggle the curve points on and off.
