what i care about

diversity + inclusion in tech

As a woman, a Latina, and a first-gen college student, I take advocacy for these communities very seriously. In the summer of 2017, I worked as a Teaching Assistant for Girls Who Code to play my part in closing the tech gender gap for the next generation of computer scientists.

human-centered design

I firmly believe that the human perspective needs to be involved in each step of the design process of a product. Humans are not robots. Our technologies should not take for granted the goals and needs of the users.

experience design

Experiences in a product should be intentional

celebrating creativity

Sometimes you just have to let yourself and your creatvity flow. I believe creativity is bravery, and ought to be celebrated.
