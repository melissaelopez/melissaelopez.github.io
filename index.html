I'M MELISSA,

A JUNIOR AT NEW YORK UNIVERSITY STUDYING COMPUTER SCIENCE AND MATH.



I spend a lot of my time in class and working on school projects, and I'm passionate about student leadership, graphic/UX design, and making sure that everyone has access to information that matters to them.

I'm originally from California, but came to school in New York City. And to be honest, it gave me a sense of independence that I didn't know I wanted (and that I probably needed) that fuels me each day. I believe firmly in equipping people with resources that make them better humans, whether that's refering first-year college students to academic resources, or showing someone a neat program that can make their lives a little easier.

I currently serve as College Captain of NYU's College of Arts and Science (CAS) College Cohort Program, and as an Ambassador for NYU Undergraduate Admissions.

PROGRAMMING

Cohort Cup Point Tracker

Cohort Cup is a competition in NYU's College of Science between its 44 first-year student cohorts. It engages 1500 students through Facebook Challenges and Marquee Events, both of which earn students points. I created this program to make tracking the scores as streamlined as possible. I'm currently working on expanding this simple Python program into an Express application.

Linker Simulator

This project was completed as part of my Operating Systems course at NYU. It's designed to simulate a two-pass linker, written in Java.

UX + PROTOTYPING

Bite

Social media users need a better way to organize their timelines/feeds across multiple networks. This prototyping project aims to solve this problem with a design for creating custom user feeds based on groups of people user's would like to follow on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram combined.

EDYou

The college admissions process is already daunting enough for any college applicant, but it can be even more difficult if you’re a first-generation student. EDYou is designed to be an admissions guide – a companion for walking you through the admissions process, providing both personalized and general task lists, deadlines, financial aid guides, and scholarship finders for any college applicant.



GRAPHIC DESIGN

#BetterSexTalk

#BetterSexTalk is a photography campaign that works to raise awareness around issues of sex education and sexual assault. Our campaign took portraits of college students across the country asking one simple question: If you could give one piece of advice about sex to a younger sibling, what would it be?

ShowSpark

ShowSpark is a mobile app that aims to make concerts more interactive by having audience members vote on components of the show, from the setlist to the stage lights.
